Location

Since 2015, ARGComFest has been based at Shoreditch Town Hall in East London. The full address is 380 Old Street, London, EC1V 9LT - although this is not a postal address for the festival team.

Transport

The nearest station to Shoreditch Town Hall is Old Street, served by the Northern line and National Rail services. Shoreditch Town Hall is around ten minutes walk from Old Street. When you arrive at Old Street, leave via exit 2 and head directly down Old Street. Shoreditch Town Hall is on the right hand side just before you reach Shoreditch High Street.

Shoreditch Town Hall is also located between two stops – Shoreditch High Street and Hoxton – on the Overground. It is around eight minutes walk from either station.

Other public transport options include the following bus routes: 26, 35, 47, 48, 55, 67, 78, 135, 149, 242 and 243.

There are a number of car parks nearby, including an NCP on Great Eastern Street - although we do not recommend travelling to the festival by car.
