Location

Since 2015, ARGComFest has taken place at Shoreditch Town Hall in East London. The full address is 380 Old Street, London, EC1V 9LT — you can find it on Google Maps, Apple Maps and Citymapper. This is not a postal address for the festival team.

Transport

The nearest station to Shoreditch Town Hall is Old Street, served by the Northern line and National Rail services. Shoreditch Town Hall is around ten minutes walk from Old Street. When you arrive at Old Street, leave via exit 2 and turn left for Old Street East (south side). Head east on Old Street, crossing Great Eastern Street to continue along Old Street. Shoreditch Town Hall is on the right hand side just before you reach Shoreditch High Street.

Shoreditch Town Hall is also located between two stops — Shoreditch High Street and Hoxton — on the Overground. It is around eight minutes walk from either station.

Other public transport options include the following bus routes: 26, 35, 47, 48, 55, 67, 78, 135, 149, 242 and 243.

The nearest NCP car park is at Finsbury Square. We do not recommend travelling to the festival by car.
