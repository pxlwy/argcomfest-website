Location

ARGComFest takes place at Shoreditch Town Hall in East London.

The full address is 380 Old Street, London, EC1V 9LT and you can find it on Google Maps, Apple Maps and Citymapper.

This is not a postal address for the festival team. If you need to contact us, please do so via email at hello@actuallyrathergood.com.

Tube / National Rail

The nearest station is Old Street, served by the Northern line (London Underground) and National Rail services. Leaving the station, you head east along Old Street — it is around a seven minute walk.

Overground

Shoreditch Town Hall is located between two Overground stops: Shoreditch High Street and Hoxton. It is around an eight minute walk from either station.

Buses

Buses stopping nearby include the 26, 35, 47, 48, 55, 67, 78, 135, 149, 242 and 243. Buses stopping directly outside the building include the 55, 243 and N55.

Driving

We do not recommend travelling to the festival by car.

Shoreditch Town Hall is located on a red route and vehicles are not allowed to stop outside the venue at any time.

The nearest NCP car park is at Finsbury Square, just under a mile away.
